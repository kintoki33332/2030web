Achievements of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

This platform highlights the achievements of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

Achievements Over the Years Since the Beginning of Vision 2030

2016 - Start of Vision

Vision 2030 was officially launched on April 25, 2016, by Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman, may God protect him.

2017 - Economic Renaissance

Increase in infrastructure projects and national economy growth.

2018 - Technological Development

Significant growth in artificial intelligence and technological innovations.

2019 - Renewable Energy Transition

Increased reliance on solar and wind energy in energy projects.

2020 - COVID-19 Pandemic

The spread of the COVID-19 pandemic and its significant impact on the economy and daily life.

2021 - Economic Recovery

The start of economic recovery after the pandemic through initiatives supporting industries.

2022 - Remote Education Advancement

The widespread adoption of remote education due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

2023 - Space Progress

The launch of new space projects and increasing interest in space exploration..

2024 - Environmental Innovations

Significant progress in environmental preservation technologies and clean energy.

National Achievements of 2024

Hosting the 2034 FIFA World Cup

In December 2024, Saudi Arabia won the bid to host the 2034 FIFA World Cup, strengthening its global sporting position.

Hosting Global Sporting Events

In 2024, Saudi Arabia hosted major global tournaments, including the FIFA Club World Cup and the eSports World Cup, reflecting its growing sporting stature.

Inauguration of Riyadh Metro

The Riyadh Metro was inaugurated on November 27, 2024, and began operations on December 1, marking a major leap in public transportation within the capital.

Archaeological Discoveries

The discovery of the ancient city of Al-Nata in November 2024, dating back to the Bronze Age (2400 BC), showcases Saudi Arabia's rich cultural heritage.

Advancement in Artificial Intelligence

The Global AI Summit was held in Riyadh in September 2024, reflecting Saudi Arabia's commitment to innovation and technology.

Enhancing Cultural Exchange

Saudi Arabia participated as the guest of honor at the 2024 Beijing International Book Fair, promoting cultural exchange between the Kingdom and the world.

Targeted Achievements for the Coming Years

Opening of the Qiddiya Project - 2025

The Qiddiya project will be the largest entertainment, sports, and cultural destination in Saudi Arabia, featuring amusement parks, museums, and sports facilities that will make the Kingdom a global destination for visitors.

Launch of the Red Sea Project - 2026

The Red Sea Project aims to develop luxury resorts along the Kingdom's coasts, including islands and beautiful beaches, to become a unique destination for international tourism.

Investment in Artificial Intelligence Technologies - 2025-2026

Saudi Arabia will continue to develop artificial intelligence technologies across various sectors such as education, healthcare, and transportation, aiming to improve services and enhance global competitiveness.

Launch of King Salman Energy City - 2027

King Salman Energy City will be a global hub for innovation in renewable energy, bringing together cutting-edge technologies in solar, wind, and water energy.

Hosting "Expo 2030" in Riyadh

Expo 2030 in Riyadh will be a global event aimed at showcasing innovations and modern technologies from around the world, while promoting international cooperation in sustainable development fields.

2029 - Launch of The Line City

The Line City will be a smart, car-free city, fully reliant on modern technology and sustainable infrastructure, reshaping the concept of urban living.
